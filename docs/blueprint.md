# **App Name**: Newton's Rarefinds

## Core Features:

- Stall Locator & Info: Displays the market location in Bury St Edmunds, operating hours (Saturday & Wednesday, 8 am - 4 pm), and contact methods for Newton's Collectables.
- Card Era & Type Showcase: A curated gallery or descriptive sections highlighting the range of Pokémon cards available, from vintage and modern sets to bulk options and rare singles, demonstrating the breadth of the collection.
- Our Story: An 'About Us' page detailing the one-year journey of Newton's Collectables, its family-friendly low-key store vibe, and the passion for Pokémon collecting.
- PokéLore Generator: An AI tool where users can input a simple prompt (e.g., 'Vintage Charizard', 'Water-type Kanto') and receive a short, descriptive lore snippet, simulating a Pokedex entry for that type of card or Pokémon.
- Event & New Arrival Spotlights: A dynamic section to announce special market days, new high-value single card arrivals, or unique bulk deals to entice visitors to the physical stall.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A clear, energetic blue (#1E9AE6), evoking modern interfaces and Water-type Pokémon, suitable for headlines and interactive elements.
- Background color: A very light, almost ethereal blue (#E9F1F5), offering a clean and expansive canvas, visibly derived from the primary hue but heavily desaturated.
- Accent color: A vibrant, sunny yellow (#F2D60F) for highlighting calls to action, important details, or creating energetic pops, reminiscent of Electric-type Pokémon and valuable cards.
- Headline and Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable design, ensuring a 'Pokédex feel' with excellent clarity and objective tone across all content.
- Clean, line-based icons, subtly incorporating abstract Pokémon motifs or elemental symbols (like fire, water, lightning) to enhance the 'Pokédex' theme without being overtly cartoony.
- A modular, card-based layout inspired by Pokedex entries, featuring clear data separation and generous use of the light background color to provide a spacious and user-friendly experience, adaptable for mobile viewing.
- Subtle UI animations such as smooth fades for content transitions, quick 'reveal' effects for information cards, and gentle hover states to provide a polished and responsive 'Pokedex' interaction.
